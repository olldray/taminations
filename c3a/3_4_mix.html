3/4 Mix

Starting formations - Right Hand Mini-Wave Box, a Diamond with the Centers in a R-H Mini-Wave, or other applicable formations.

Those who can Right Arm Turn 3/4; those in a Line/Wave Mix. Ends in a Line or Wave.

3/4 Mix is a 3-part call: Right Arm Turn 3/4; Centers Cross Run; New Centers Trade.
