Checkerboard Anything

Starting formation - 2 x 4 (or other applicable formations) with precisely two Leaders in each Line.

In the following diagrams, "L" designates the Leaders in each Line as "O" designates the other dancers. The dancers designated by "O" can be facing in any direction other than the same direction as the dancers designated "L" in their 1 x 4.

The Leaders in each Line ("L" dancers) Trade with each other as the others ("O" dancers) do the Anything call working in a distorted 1 x 4. Ends in a 2 x 4.
