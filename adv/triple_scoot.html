Triple Scoot

From Columns. Dancers facing each other on a diagonal (three pairs) step forward to join adjacent forearms, turn one-half (180°) and step forward to finish in a position vacated by the dancer who was originally adjacent to them in the column. Meanwhile, the #1 dancer in each column Runs into the position vacated by the adjacent #4 dancer who is doing the forearm turn. When done from right-hand columns, the dancers facing in turn by the right. When done from left-hand columns, the dancers facing in turn by the left hand.

Timing: 6
